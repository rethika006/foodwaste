About Food Waste Management

Food Waste Management is a scalable web platform built to reduce waste and improve food distribution.

Mission

Reduce food waste across communities, create equitable access, and support local NGOs in distribution and tracking.

Vision

A future where no edible food is discarded, and surplus is redirected to families who need it most.

Features

  • Role-based portals: Donor, Receiver, NGO.
  • Food listing with image and expiry data.
  • Request flow + accept/reject + delivery status tracking.
  • Admin panel control and user management.

How to Start

Create an account, post donations, and help your community by matching food supply and demand.
